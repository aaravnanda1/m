Miraculous Ladybug - Episode 2

Lady Wifi

When Alya gets suspended from school because of Chloé, she becomes akumatized into the villain Lady Wifi. Determined to expose Ladybug’s identity, Lady Wifi uses her smartphone to freeze people in place and take control of technology.

Will Marinette be able to protect her secret identity while defeating Lady Wifi alongside Cat Noir? Find out in this thrilling episode!
